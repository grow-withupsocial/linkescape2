What Are In-App Browsers?

Apps like WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Telegram, and TikTok have built-in browsers that open links instead of sending them to your default browser. These in-app browsers:

  • • Can't access your password managers
  • • Have limited functionality
  • • Slow down your browsing
  • • Track your activity extensively

LinkEscape solves this by showing users a page with a button to open in their default browser.
